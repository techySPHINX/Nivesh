## Problem

### User Problems

1. **Scattered Financial Data**  
   People have financial data across apps/accounts but no single place that turns it into clear decisions.

2. **No Decision Intelligence**  
   Existing finance apps show charts and budgets but don't answer real-life "can I afford this?" questions.

3. **Planning Overwhelm**  
   Financial planning requires expertise; most users feel overwhelmed, anxious, and unsure.

4. **Costly Uninformed Decisions**  
   Users make costly decisions (loans, investments, lifestyle upgrades) without understanding long-term impact.

### Existing Alternatives

**How these problems are solved today:**

- **Budgeting apps:** expense tracking but no financial intelligence
- **Mutual fund / broker apps:** product-focused, not holistic life planning
- **Excel templates:** powerful but manual, time-consuming, and error-prone
- **Human advisors:** expensive, not always trustworthy, and not available 24/7
- **Asking friends/family:** traditional recommendations, but subjective and not always practical

---

## Solution

### Core Solutions

1. **AI Financial Conversations**  
   Ask questions in simple language and get direct answers + visuals.

2. **Goal-based Strategy Planning**  
   Converts life goals into step-by-step savings/investing roadmaps.

3. **Scenario Simulation Engine**  
   "What-if" simulations (loan vs rent, SIP increase, job change, emergency event).

4. **Smart Alerts & Interventions**  
   Detects anomalies + warns users before damage happens (overspending, under-saving, risky allocation).

## Customer Segments

### Target Customers

1. **Young professionals (22–35)**  
   Salaried, new investors, planning big milestones.

2. **Middle-income families (30–50)**  
   Managing expenses, home loans, education planning, retirement.

3. **High-intent finance users**  
   Already using SIPs, mutual funds, credit cards—need smarter decisions.

4. **NRIs / globally mobile users**  
   Multi-currency planning, goal alignment, structured strategy.

### Early Adopters

**Characteristics of ideal customers:**

- 1st job earners who want clear investing guidance beyond influencers
- People actively planning home loans / marriage / higher studies within 12–36 months
- Users frustrated with tracking apps and looking for a financial strategist not a tracker
- Finance-curious Gen Z & Millennials comfortable with AI tools

## Key Metrics

### Key Activities to Measure

1. **Weekly Active Users (WAU) and retention (D7 / D30)**

2. **Decision time reduction**  
   How quickly users get clarity/answers

3. **Scenario simulations per user**  
   Weekly engagement depth

4. **Goal plan completion rate**  
   Onboarding → strategy setup

5. **Premium conversion rate**  
   Free → paid

6. **Financial health uplift**  
   Higher savings rate, fewer overdrafts, improved portfolio allocation
